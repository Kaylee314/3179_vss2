Alcohol Consumption

Alcohol can be found in beer, wine and spirits which causes people drunkenness. But in moderation, alcohol can act as a stimulant, making people cheerful and talkative. For example, people will drink in some parties for social, celebreting and relaxing.

Different alcoholic drinks contain different alcohol content. For example, the alcohol content of red wine is about 12%, while that of spirits is 40% and that of beer is only 5%. Therefore, reported in liters of pure alcohol.
